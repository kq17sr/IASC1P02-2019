About Us

Kids Coding Academy offers coding programs to educate/inspire youth ages 10-25. We teach students how to develop websites and video games. We provide an enriched learning environment that has helped countless students advance. We are located in St. Catharines, Canada where our environment reflects the diverse backgrounds and cultures of the area. We are extremely proud of our students, who are always eager to learn, create and grow together. Are you ready to start your future with us?
